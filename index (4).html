S Durian World：自2025年起构建榴莲的未来S Durian World: Building the Future of Durian Since 2025

S Durian World 是一家创新型榴莲企业，致力于塑造榴莲产业的未来。我们拥有吉兰丹瓜拉姆沙洛景高原2,000英亩的优质土地——猫山王的原产地之一，提供理想的榴莲种植和热带农业旅游条件。迄今为止，已在200英亩土地上种植了超过10,000棵猫山王榴莲树。

S Durian World is an innovative durian-focused enterprise committed to shaping the future of the durian industry. We own 2,000 acres of prime land in the Lojing Highlands, Kelantan — one of the native regions of Musang King — offering ideal conditions for durian cultivation and tropical agrotourism. Over 10,000 Musang King trees have been planted across 200 acres.

洛景高原的"波尔多"：我们的猫山王与众不同The "Bordeaux" of Musang King: Why Our Durian Stands Alone

🏔️

高海拔优势High Altitude Advantage

较凉爽的空气使榴莲生长更慢，风味更浓郁，口感更丰富。The cooler highland air makes durians grow slower, creating richer, more complex flavour profiles.

🌱

独特土壤Perfect Soil Composition

洛景高原独特的土壤赋予榴莲无可替代的独特口味，无法在别处复制。The unique soil of Lojing Highlands gives durian a taste that cannot be replicated anywhere else on earth.

☀️

理想气候Ideal Climate

完美的雨水与阳光组合，造就超级奶油质感、苦甜适中、无水味的榴莲。The perfect mix of rain and sun creates a super creamy, bitter-sweet durian with zero watery taste.

🎯

正宗原产地Authentic Origin

并非所有猫山王都来自瓜拉姆沙——只有洛景高原的才能提供这种难忘的味觉体验。Not all Musang King is equal — only those from Lojing Highlands of Gua Musang offer this once-in-a-lifetime experience.
